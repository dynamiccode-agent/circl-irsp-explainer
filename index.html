Platform Overview

Insurance Replacement Service Platform

CIRCL IRSP connects builders, insurers, and homeowners to manage energy-efficient appliance replacement jobs under insurance schemes. Claims flow from lodgement through eligibility checking, job allocation, installation, and customer authority to closure.

🏗️
Builder Portal — Circl Operator
Builders lodge claims, track the replacement pipeline, assign jobs to trade installers, and manage sustainability reporting.

Authentication

Sign Up & Sign In

Authentication is handled by Clerk. Users belong to organisations — a builder org, an insurer org, or the platform admin org. Your role within your organisation determines which portal and features you can access.

platform_admin
Platform Admin
Full access to everything. Manages schemes, products, orgs, and platform settings. Can see all claims across all orgs.
builder_admin
Builder Admin
Full access within their builder org — claims, jobs, team, sustainability reporting.
builder_operator
Builder Operator
Creates and manages claims and jobs within their org. Cannot manage team members.
trade_installer
Trade Installer
Sees jobs assigned to them. Updates job status and submits completion evidence.
insurer_admin
Insurer Admin
Full read access to their insurer org's claims, rebates, and carbon data.
insurer_viewer
Insurer Viewer
Read-only access to claims for their insurer organisation.

Builder Portal · Customer Portal

Customer Authority

Before a replacement order can be submitted, the customer must grant rebate authority. The builder sends a secure access link from the claim detail sidebar. The customer receives a unique URL — no account required — and either grants or refuses authority from a minimal portal page.

1

Builder clicks "Send Access Link" in the claim sidebar

This creates a 32-byte hex token tied to the customer with a 14-day expiry window and triggers the notification.

2

Customer receives the link by email or SMS

URL format: circl.app/c/[32-byte-token] — opens without any login prompt.

3

Customer reviews and grants or refuses authority

The portal shows the property, replacement products selected, and a rebate authority form. Signing moves the claim to Fulfilment Scheduled. Refusing captures a refusal reason and moves to Authority Refused.

The Customer Authority card in the claim detail sidebar shows the current status (Pending / Signed / Refused) and updates in real-time once the customer acts.
Insurer Portal

Insurer Portal

Insurer users have four sections: Dashboard (open claims, monthly rebates, carbon position), Claims (full list, read-only), Sustainability (carbon tokens issued across the program), and Rebates (government rebate certificates). No write access — all fulfilment is managed by the builder.
